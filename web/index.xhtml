CheerLeading Sports Information Systems

This information system optimizes the administrative processes of the Cheerleading club, allowing a better organization, easy management and its visualization, helping to solve the company´s needs of growth, making organizational, productive and technological changes, with the sole purpose of becoming more efficient so it can achieve a better position in the market.

What we're looking for, what we want and what we do

MISSION AND VISION

MISSION

Information system with web interface, which optimizes the administrative processes of the Cheerleading club, allowing better organization, easy management and visualization. helping to solve the need for the growth of the company, making organizational, productive and technological changes, with the sole purpose of making it more efficient and can achieve a better position in the market at a general level and compete in the future.

VISION

To be recognized as a prestigious information system, at the regional, national and international level, in the area of cheerleading, information systems, technologies associated with the training and excellence of its athletes, managing to reach all cheerleading clubs and sponsoring entities facilitating the management, teaching and implementation of their athletes to improve their sporting position in competitions.

OBJETIVES

Record and control profiles and pathological history.

Allow you to design,edit, and assign training plans .

Facilitate evaluation information in training results.

Generate control of club sports materials nationwide.
